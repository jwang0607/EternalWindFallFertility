Who Are Our Surrogates?

Our surrogates are exceptional women who have chosen to help others experience the joy of parenthood. They come from diverse backgrounds but share common traits: compassion, responsibility, and a genuine desire to make a difference in the lives of intended parents.

All surrogates in our program undergo comprehensive screening, including medical, psychological, and background evaluations to ensure they're ready for this meaningful journey.

What Makes an Ideal Surrogate

Compassionate

Our surrogates have a genuine desire to help others build their families.

Experienced Mothers

All our surrogates have successfully carried and delivered at least one child.

Healthy Lifestyle

They maintain excellent physical and mental health throughout the journey.

Committed

They're fully dedicated to the surrogacy process from beginning to end.
