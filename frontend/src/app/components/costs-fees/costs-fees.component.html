Surrogacy Costs & Considerations

Understanding the full financial picture of your surrogacy journey

Medical Expenses

These include fertility clinic fees, medications, embryo creation and storage, prenatal care, delivery, and post-birth medical care. Some of these costs may be covered by insurance.

$45,000 - $75,000

Legal Fees

Legal representation for intended parents and surrogate, contracts, establishment of parentage, and any immigration considerations for international clients.

$8,000 - $20,000

Surrogate Compensation

Base compensation for the surrogate, plus additional payments for medical procedures, lost wages, travel expenses, and maternity clothing.

$35,000 - $50,000

Insurance

Health insurance for the surrogate and the baby during pregnancy and delivery, life insurance for the surrogate, and any supplemental insurance as needed.

$15,000 - $30,000

Travel & Accommodation

Expenses for travel to the fertility clinic, prenatal appointments, and birth, including airfare, lodging, and meals.

$5,000 - $15,000

Newborn Care

Hospital expenses for the newborn, pediatric care, and any necessary newborn specialists or NICU care if required.

Variable
